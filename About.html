MAYENDA DA/SDA BASIC SCHOOL

Mayenda DA/SDA Basic School is located in Awutu Mayenda, a community in the Awutu Senya West district.

We are in the Ayensuako Circuit together with some surrounding sister schools. We share boundaries with the Eastern region.

The school was established in the nineties and many prominent citizens had their basic education here. Mayenda is a small

farming community which takes keen delight in the education of her members, especially children of all ages.

Currently, the school has pupils not from only Mayenda but from other neighbouring communties like Kawoanopaado,

Takyiman, Nyame B3kyer3 and Abowankyewonwe. Over the years the school has seen several progress in her

quest to fulfilling her mission. Our doors are open to all who wish to attain basic education!
